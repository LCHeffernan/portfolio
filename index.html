About

I'm Lisa and I am seeking a career change from teaching to software engineering. Ever since I started school I have loved maths and solving problems. When I started coding at university in my undergraduate degree in maths I learnt how to program in FORTRAN. During my Masters in fluid dynamics I continued to write in FORTRAN for many projects. For my dissertation I taught myself C++ and wrote a program to track diffusion across two different fluids. I went on to become a maths teacher in secondary schools but I am ready for a new challenge and want to become a software engineer. Manchester codes has reignited my passion for coding. I have loved learning everything on the course. So far I have learnt CLI, GitHub, HTML, CSS, Javascript, node.js, express.js, SQL, Sequelize, Docker and React.
